What is Gold Coast Coworking

The Gold Coast Co-Working initiative has been set up as a hub for Gold Coast IT professionals to work and collaborate.

Rather then work from home or coffee shops, come work in a creative and collaborative environment sharing knowledge and experience with a range of friendly individuals including entrepreneurs, designers, programmers, engineers and more.

Gold Coast Coworking is partnered with the Gold Coast TechSpace. Collectively they are a not-for-profit venture with all membership fees going back into the space.

Coworking Membership fees are $200.00 p/m. This includes facilities, wifi, desks, chairs in a location surrounded by multiple coffee shop and lunch options. Some members choose to pop in only one or two days a week, others work here permanently - It's up to you!!
