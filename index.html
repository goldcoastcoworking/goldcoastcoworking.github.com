What is Gold Coast CoWorking

The Gold Coast CoWorking is a hub for Gold Coast IT & Start up Professionals including founders, developers and designers to work and collaborate.

Why CoWork?

Meet new people and get your work done in a professional environment. Need someone to bounce ideas or ask a question, you'll be surrounded by the right people.

Facilities & Price

$200.00 p/m Per month. That's It!

Includes:

  • Wifi / Desk / Chair / Tea / Coffee
  • 24/7 access
  • Multiple coffee shop and lunch options
  • Free Car Parking
  • Awesome lunch conversations
  • Latest movie posters hanging on the wall
  • $2 Steaks on Fridays at the local

Some members choose to pop in only one or two days a week, others work here permanently - It's up to you!!

Gold Coast Serviced Offices

 

Unlike standard Serviced Offices which are expensive and stick you in a small lonely cubical to spend your day, Gold Coast CoWorking offers a friendly and open floor plan, where you can work privately on your project as well as collaborate with others.

Gold Coast CoWorking also offers private rooms to work in by yourself or with a small team.

Note: Because we generally keep a quite and professional work environment, we are not suitable for someone on the phone all day or for a place to bring regular clients.
